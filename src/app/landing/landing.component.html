Cleanup

It is recommended to cleanup all pre-generated demo contents before project development starts. Follow next steps:

  1. Go to src/app/app-routing.module.ts
  2. Delete the imports for LandingComponent and DemoComponent
  3. Delete the routes objects inside the "routes" constant
  4. Go to src/app/app.module.ts
  5. Delete the imports for LandingModule and DemoModule
  6. Delete LandingModule and DemoModule from AppModule imports' array
  7. Delete the "landing" and "demo" feature areas from scr/app
